# ValiChord — Funder Summary

ValiChord is a distributed validation infrastructure designed to strengthen scientific reproducibility at scale. It provides a structured, auditable, and decentralised mechanism for independently verifying scientific claims before they enter the literature or inform policy.

## The Problem

Despite major investments in open science, the research ecosystem still lacks:
- reliable, independent validation of scientific findings  
- transparent governance structures for assessing methodological quality  
- scalable mechanisms for coordinating multi‑site verification  
- incentives for researchers to prioritise reproducibility  

This gap undermines trust, slows innovation, and leads to costly irreproducible results.

## The ValiChord Solution

ValiChord introduces a harmonised framework that integrates:
- **Distributed Validation** — independent validators assess claims using standardised protocols  
- **Transparent Governance** — clear roles, responsibilities, and decision‑making structures  
- **Technical Infrastructure** — workflows, documentation, and audit trails that scale across disciplines  
- **Alignment with Registered Reports** — ensuring methodological rigour before data collection  

ValiChord acts as a “validation layer” for science: a system that sits alongside journals, funders, and institutions to ensure claims are robust before they propagate.

## Why This Matters

ValiChord delivers:
- higher confidence in published findings  
- reduced waste from irreproducible research  
- improved accountability and transparency  
- a scalable model that can be adopted across disciplines  
- a clear pathway for funders to support reproducibility without overburdening researchers  

## What Funding Enables

Investment will support:
- development of the technical infrastructure  
- governance hardening and community consultation  
- pilot programmes with partner institutions  
- integration with Registered Reports and existing open‑science platforms  
- outreach, training, and validator recruitment  

## Vision

ValiChord aims to establish a new epistemic infrastructure for science — one that harmonises methodological rigour, transparent governance, and distributed validation. The long‑term goal is to position Wales and the UK as global leaders in research integrity and reproducibility.
